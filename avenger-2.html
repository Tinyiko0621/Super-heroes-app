Tony Stark is the wealthy son of industrialist and weapons manufacturer Howard Stark and his wife, Maria. Tony grew up a genius with a brilliant mind for technology and inventions and, naturally, followed in his father's footsteps, inheriting Stark Industries upon his parents’ untimely death.

He designed advanced weapons of war for Stark Industries while living the lifestyle of a bon vivant. But fate took a dark turn when he was captured overseas during a demonstration and nearly killed by a bomb made with his own tech.

While imprisoned by the warlord Wong-Chu, fellow captive Yinsen saved Tony’s life by building a device to keep shrapnel from reaching his heart. Inspired, Tony secretly built the first Iron Man suit to escape and start his path as a hero.
