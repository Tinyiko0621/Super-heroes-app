Golden Archer

Very little is known about the S.H.I.E.L.D. agent known as Hawkeye or his early life. An accomplished marksman and weapons master, he played a role in Thor’s arrival on Earth and was key to bringing Natasha Romanoff (Black Widow) into S.H.I.E.L.D.

Because of his unmatched skills, Hawkeye was recruited into the Avengers Initiative by Nick Fury. Though his first mission under Loki’s mind control had him fighting against the team, he quickly regained control and helped stop the Asgardian trickster.

Since then, Hawkeye has stood against the Chitauri, Ultron, HYDRA, and even his fellow Avengers when duty demanded it. His loyalty, precision, and calm under pressure make him an irreplaceable member of the team.
