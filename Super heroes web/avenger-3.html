Not Easy Being Green

Bioscience genius Dr. Bruce Banner focused his studies on gamma radiation, working alongside Dr. Betty Ross at a Virginia University lab. Unbeknownst to him, her father, General "Thunderbolt" Ross, redirected Banner's work for military use. When Banner tested his work on himself, it triggered a dramatic transformation—turning him into a hulking, rage-powered creature.

The Hulk destroyed the lab and injured Ross, forcing Banner to flee and begin a desperate search for a cure to his affliction. On the run, he became both legend and target, trying to avoid detection while also battling the beast within.

Nobody Stronger

While Bruce Banner is one of the most brilliant minds on Earth, his alter ego—the Hulk—is unmatched in raw power. The Hulk can leap vast distances, survive extreme damage, and lift weights beyond measurement. His strength only grows with his rage.

Over time, Hulk has evolved from a mindless brute to a creature capable of basic speech and reasoning. Banner often refers to him as “the other guy,” and their uneasy relationship remains central to both man and monster.
